Privacy Policy

Last updated: 04 March 2026

1. Information we collect

We collect account information (such as email and profile details), user-generated content (pet profiles, stories, comments, and messages), and technical data required to operate and secure the platform.

2. How we use information

We use collected data to provide core app functionality, show content to users, support rankings and engagement features, prevent abuse, and improve service quality.

3. Likes, rankings, and visibility

Likes on pets and stories are used to calculate total and weekly ranking results, including Star of the Week displays for cats and dogs.

4. Storage and third-party services

Paw Star uses Supabase services for authentication, database storage, and media storage. Uploaded images and content are processed and stored through these infrastructure services.

5. Data retention

We retain account and content data while your account is active or as needed to provide the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our terms.

6. Your choices

You can update profile information, edit or remove your content where supported, and contact us for account-related privacy requests.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at info@pawstar.com.
