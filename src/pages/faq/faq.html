Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about accounts, pets, stories, likes, comments, messaging, and rankings.

Open the Register page, fill in your details, and submit the form. After registration you can log in and start creating pet profiles and stories.

Yes. Logged-in users can create multiple pet profiles and manage each profile with its own photo and description.

You can create stories for your pets and add tags to help other users discover them. Tags and keywords are used in search to find relevant stories.

Each pet receives likes from other users. Paw Star tracks total likes and weekly likes. Weekly likes reset at the start of each new week (Monday to Sunday cycle).

Every week, one cat and one dog with the highest weekly likes are highlighted as "Star of the Week" and shown at the top of the ranking.

Commenting and direct messaging are available for authenticated users. This helps keep communication accountable and safer for the community.
